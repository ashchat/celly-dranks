Single-Serving Recipes For All Four

1 — Hugo Spritz

  • 3 oz Prosecco
  • 2 oz St. Germain (elderflower liqueur)
  • 1 oz club soda
  • Fresh mint sprig, lime wheel
  • Ice
  1. Fill a wine glass or tumbler with ice.
  2. Pour in the Prosecco and St. Germain.
  3. Top with club soda — pour gently to keep the bubbles.
  4. One light stir, no more.
  5. Garnish with mint and lime. Done.

2 — Green Card Margarita

  • 2 oz mezcal or tequila
  • 1 oz blue curaçao
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • 0.5 oz jalapeño-agave syrup*
  • Tajín, for the rim
  1. Rim a rocks glass with lime juice and Tajín.
  2. Shake mezcal, curaçao, lime juice, and syrup hard with ice.
  3. Strain over fresh ice in the rimmed glass.
  4. *For the syrup: simmer equal parts sugar and water with a few sliced jalapeños for 5 min, cool, strain.

3 — Firework Cubes

  • 1 small box (3oz) flavored Jell-O (red or blue)
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1 cup cold vodka
  • 1/2 packet unflavored gelatin
  • 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk
  1. Dissolve the flavored Jell-O in boiling water, stir in cold vodka, pour into a shallow pan, chill 2-3 hrs until firm, then cube.
  2. Bloom the unflavored gelatin in a splash of cold water, dissolve gently, whisk into the condensed milk with a splash of vodka.
  3. Drop cubes into small cups, pour the white mixture over, chill 1-2 hrs until set.

4 — Espresso Float

  • 1.5 oz vodka
  • 1 oz coffee liqueur
  • 1 oz strong instant decaf, chilled
  • 1 scoop vanilla or cinnamon ice cream
  1. Dissolve 1-2 tsp instant decaf in a splash of hot water, let cool completely.
  2. Scoop ice cream into a coupe or rocks glass.
  3. Shake vodka, coffee liqueur, and cooled decaf hard with ice.
  4. Pour over the ice cream tableside so it foams up like a float.
